Welcome to the Haven

Residential living for college students in recovery from substance use disorder

Together, we do what we can’t do apart. From this simple notion the Haven was born. The Haven provides a
community to navigate both mid-terms and life’s terms. We are an “extended family” that can identify with
each other’s challenges and help each other to reach real solutions. At the Haven we focus on keeping it fun,
keeping each other safe and getting the absolute most from college life – clean and sober.
